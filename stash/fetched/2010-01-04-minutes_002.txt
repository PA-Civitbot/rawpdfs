1.    Election of Mayor and Vice Mayor

MOTION: Council Member Klein nominated Council Member Burt for Mayor.

Council Member Klein spoke regarding his nomination of Council Member
Burt for Mayor for 2010. He spoke about his accomplishments that include
being a star athlete at University of California-Santa Barbara,
Founder/President of Acteron which has won numerous awards for clean
technology. He spoke about his years as a Commissioner on the Planning
and Transportation Commission.

At this point a paper ballot was conducted.
Council Member Holman stated her support of the nomination. She stated
she has experienced his expertise, knowledge and dedication to public
process while serving with him on the Planning & Transportation
Commission. She expects the new Mayor to lead the Council with further
transparency, open government and a proactive approach when having to
deal with project evaluation process.

Council Member Scharff stated his support of the nomination. He stated that
he believed Council Member Burt as Mayor would be thoughtful, insightful,
and listen to all sides on every issue.
