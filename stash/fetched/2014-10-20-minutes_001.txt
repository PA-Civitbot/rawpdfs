STUDY SESSION

1.   Joint Study Session of the         City   Council   and   Planning    and
     Transportation Commission.

Mark Michael, Planning and Transportation Commission Chair, hoped to
explore some practical ideas related to the land use and planning process.
The Planning and Transportation Commission (P&TC) served the Council as
an advisory body and wanted to understand if it could improve its service to
the Council. Director Gitelman posed four questions for discussion regarding
the P&TC's support of the Council and the P&TC's role in providing a forum
for public engagement. He suggested a Staff Report regarding land use and
planning matters include a section concisely related to Motions, votes, key
points raised in discussion, and substantive public comments.          When
necessary and appropriate, Staff and the P&TC could submit an executive
summary to serve as a reference for the Council.          If a land use or
