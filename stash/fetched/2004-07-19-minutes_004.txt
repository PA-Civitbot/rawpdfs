because she was on the Board of the Community Working Group, which
created and oversees the Opportunity Center.

Council Member Freeman registered a no vote on Item No. 8.

Community Environment Steve Emslie served in a non-salaried position on
the InVision Board of Directors, a non-profit corporation, and decided not to
participate in negotiating agreements or forming the staff recommendations
for Item No. 7.
