The City Council met on this date in the Council Conference Room at 6:01

           Klein, Kniss, Scharff, Schmid, Shepherd

ORAL COMMUNICATIONS

Shani Kleinhaus remarked on the eradication of squirrels at Byxbee Park and
discussed how the City of Berkeley addressed this issue; she requested staff
cease the eradication of squirrels. She wanted to remove a portion of the
landfill and preserve the wildlife.
