MOTION: Council Member DuBois moved, seconded by Council Member

Environment Element with the addition of a Goal of Climate Change and
Adaptation, moving Goals on Natural Hazards (Goal N-2), and Hazardous
Waste (Goal N-6), and Solid Waste (Goal N-7) to the Safety Element and
updating the Goal of Noise (Goal N-8) to include airplane noise.

AMENDMENT: Vice Mayor Schmid moved, seconded by Council Member XX
