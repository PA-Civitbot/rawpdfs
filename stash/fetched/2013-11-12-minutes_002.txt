CITY MANAGER COMMENTS

James Keene, City Manager, reported that Palo Alto was named the Number
1 Digital City in America by the Center for Digital Government. Aurora, a
life-size tree sculpture, would be unveiled at King Plaza on November 16,
2013. The Fiscal Year 2014 Asphalt Paving Project would repave 45 blocks
as a result of increased funding.

COUNCIL MEMBER QUESTIONS, COMMENTS, AND ANNOUNCEMENTS

Council Member Schmid hoped the Council would have a revision of the Palo
Alto Traffic Model and an updated parking study prior to discussing the two
large development projects noted on tentative Council Agendas.

Council Member Price, as co-chair of the Families, Adolescents and Children
Committee of the Santa Clara County Mental Health Board, invited the public
to attend a panel discussion regarding services for transitioning youth on
November 14, 2013, in San Jose.

Vice Mayor Shepherd inquired about the procedure for addressing the
Maybell Project after the Measure D election.
