SPECIAL ORDERS OF THE DAY

1.    Election of Mayor and Vice Mayor

MOTION: Mayor Kishimoto moved, seconded by Council Member Espinosa,
to nominate Council Member Larry Klein as Mayor.

Mayor Kishimoto nominated Larry Klein as Mayor for his leadership,
experience, hard work, fresh energy and passion for all of the important
issues that come to face the Council.

Council Member Espinosa nominated Larry Klein for his strong record of
leadership.

MOTION PASSED: 9-0.

Mayor Klein thanked his family, the Dignitaries in attendance, Board and
Commission Members for their attendance and support of the City. He
discussed items to be accomplished during his term; a City Manager, the
Environmental Protection Plan, the upgrading of the library system, the
Public Safety Building, and the Stanford Medical/Shopping Center expansion.

Council Member Espinosa nominated, seconded by Council Member Yeh to
nominate Council Member John Barton as Vice Mayor.

Council Member Burt nominated, seconded by Council Member Kishimoto to
nominate Council Member Peter Drekmeier as Vice Mayor.

At this point a paper ballot was conducted.

Council Member Yeh nominated Council Member Barton for his vast
experience in effective business leadership and locating key players in official
capacities.
