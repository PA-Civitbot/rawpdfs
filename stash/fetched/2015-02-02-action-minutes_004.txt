                     ACTION MINUTES
SUBSTITUTE MOTION: Council Member DuBois moved, seconded by Mayor
Holman to adopt a Resolution to schedule the City Council’s summer break
from July 13, 2015 to August 14, 2015.

SUBSTITUTE MOTION FAILED: 3-4 DuBois, Filseth, Holman yes, Burt,
Wolbach absent

MOTION PASSED: 6-1 Holman no, Burt, Wolbach absent

MOTION: Council Member Scharff moved, seconded by Council Member
Kniss to set the Council’s winter closure from December 21, 2015 to January 1,
2016.

MOTION PASSED: 7-0 Burt, Wolbach absent

Council Member Questions, Comments and Announcements

Council Member Kniss commented on the success of the City Council Retreat
on January 31, 2015.

Mayor Holman reported that she, Council Member Burt and Council Member
Kniss attended the Canopy annual awards on January 29, 2015 at the
Rinconada Library. Mayor Holman planted a Ginko tree as part of the
traditional Mayor’s tree planting during the event. She enjoyed a sneak peak
of the new library.

Adjournment: The meeting was adjourned at 9:56 P.M.
