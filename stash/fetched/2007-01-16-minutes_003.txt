ORAL COMMUNICATIONS

Mark Petersen-Perez, 434 Addison Avenue, spoke regarding the Taser
International Annual Report. He requested the Council dismantle the Taser
Task Force.

Herb Borock, P.O. Box 632, spoke regarding the ultra high speed broadband
Request for Proposals (RFP) evaluation process.

Kathy Miller, 849 Lincoln Avenue, said the Children’s Library fundraising
efforts were a success. She presented the City with $400,000 of private
funds to furnish the Children’s Library.

Aram James requested the Council reject the Police Department’s request to
purchase tasers. He reiterated it was imperative the public have accessibility
to City records.

APPROVAL OF MINUTES
