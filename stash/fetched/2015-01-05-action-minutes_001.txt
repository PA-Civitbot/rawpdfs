              CITY OF PALO ALTO CITY COUNCIL
              ACTION MINUTES
                                                               Regular Meeting
                                                               January 5, 2015

The City Council of the City of Palo Alto met on this date in the Council
Chambers at 6:15 P.M.

Present:    Berman, Burt, Holman, Klein, Kniss, Price, Scharff, Schmid,
Shepherd

Absent:

MOTION: Council Member Holman moved, seconded by Council Member
Burt to move Agenda Item Number 6 - City Manager Year in Review, forward
to be heard before Agenda Item Number 1.

MOTION PASSED: 6-0 Klein, Price, Shepherd not participating

Acting City Clerk, Beth Minor swears in Council Members Tom DuBois, Eric
Filseth, Cory Wolbach, Karen Holman and Greg Scharff.

Special Orders of the Day

1.    Election of the Mayor for 2015.

NOMINATION: Vice Mayor Kniss nominated Council Member Holman for
Mayor for 2015.

NOMINATION PASSED: 9-0

2.   Election of the Vice Mayor for 2015.

NOMINATION: Council Member Scharff nominated Council Member Schmid
for Vice Mayor for 2015.

NOMINATION: Council Member Filseth nominated Council Member Burt for
Vice Mayor for 2015.
