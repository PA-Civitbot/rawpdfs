SPECIAL ORDERS OF THE DAY

1.   Resolution 8550 entitled “Resolution of the Council of the City of Palo
     Alto Expressing Appreciation to Don Greenwell Upon His Retirement”

Police Chief Lynne Johnson said Mr. Greenwell worked in the Palo Alto Police
Department from 1969 to 1980. In 1980, he transferred to Poke County,
Oregon, returned to Palo Alto in 1983, and was promoted to Chief Dispatcher.
She thanked him for his accomplishments and dedication to the City. Since
1983, Mr. Greenwell commuted daily from Manteca to Palo Alto.

Don Greenwell thanked the citizens of Palo Alto and said it was an honor and
a privilege being a dispatcher for so many years.

MOTION: Council Member Beecham moved, seconded by Kleinberg, to
approve the Resolution.

MOTION PASSED 8-0, Ojakian absent.
