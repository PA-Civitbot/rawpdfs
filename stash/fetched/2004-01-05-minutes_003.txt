SPECIAL ORDERS OF THE DAY

1.   Election of Mayor and Vice Mayor

Council Member Kishimoto nominated Vice Mayor Bern Beecham for Mayor.
She felt he was qualified for the position because of his knowledge on City
issues, his many years of service with the Planning and Transportation
Commission (P&TC), he had been a Council Member for the past four years,
and he served as Vice Mayor in 2003. He was the right person to guide staff
and Council and would be a good leader for the community.

MOTION: Council Member Morton moved, seconded by Ojakian, to appoint
Vice Mayor Beecham for Mayor by acclamation.

MOTION PASSED 9-0.

Council Member Mossar congratulated Bern Beecham as the new Mayor.

Council Member Morton nominated his colleague and mentor Council
Member Jim Burch for Vice Mayor. Council Member Burch made a decision
40 years prior to devote his energy to serving his community and looked
forward to his leadership as Vice Mayor.

MOTION: Council Member Mossar moved, seconded by Ojakian, to appoint
Council Member Burch for Vice Mayor by acclamation.

MOTION PASSED 9-0.

Mayor Bern Beecham congratulated Jim Burch as the new Vice Mayor.

Mayor Pro Tem David Casas, Los Altos, congratulated both the new Mayor
and Vice Mayor and wished them the best in the coming year.

Mayor Beecham thanked his colleagues for selecting him as Mayor for 2004.
