Retreat Discussion

1.      Governing Together.

        A.   This session will be a facilitated conversation between the Council
             and to some extent the Council Appointed Officers. Executive
             Leadership Team members will not attend. Council will discuss how
             to govern together most effectively, roles and frameworks for
             effectiveness, and how to best accomplish the work of the Council
             and the staff.
