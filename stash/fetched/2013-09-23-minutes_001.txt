SPECIAL ORDERS OF THE DAY

2.   Presentation by Santa Clara County District Attorney Jeff Rosen.

Jeff Rosen, District Attorney, explained that the mission of the District
Attorney's Office was to aggressively pursue justice while treating everyone
fairly, equally and with respect. The core values were service, hard work,
transparency and integrity. The District Attorney's Office employed 540
people. The crime laboratory was state of the art and internationally
accredited. Tours of the crime laboratory were available. The District
Attorney's Office prosecuted approximately 40,000 criminal cases a year, the
majority of which were misdemeanor cases.
